Quinn Trading Engine

Private forex trading automation and account monitoring tool.

Quinn Trading Engine is a private forex trading automation and account-monitoring tool owned and operated by An Truong.

The application is used only by the owner for personal cTrader trading accounts. It connects through the cTrader Open API to read authorized account data, monitor positions, and execute trades only on accounts owned and authorized by the application owner.

Quinn Trading Engine is not a public trading service, not a broker, not an investment adviser, and does not manage funds or accounts for third parties.
